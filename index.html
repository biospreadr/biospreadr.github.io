Bringing automation to biomedical laboratories.

Mission Statement

As demand for microbiological testing increases by up to 15% each year, labs struggle to satisfy this need. While labs in other fields have long since benefited from the use of laboratory automation, microbiology labs continue to significantly lag their peers. BioSpreadr aims to improve efficiency and consistency in microbiology labs by automating and parallelizing the sample preparation process. BioSpread uses innovative components designed from the ground up to ensure that every Petri dish is quickly and consistently prepared.
